Dynamic Power Solutions Sdn Bhd is a leading Mechanical & Electrical System Solutions Provider Company specialized in the field of electrical & mechanical system installation involving HT, LV & ELV, generating plant or emergency power supply system, switchboard, air conditioning system, fire suppression system, noise attenuation system

The company aims to provide the highest level of quality for its technical services by continuing to develop and apply efficient techniques. This makes the company one of the best system solutions providers in the region. In all of the projects we take on, we define two distinct areas that must be properly handled and administered, those are ‘Project Management” and “Project Implementation”. Project management involves cost budgeting, planning, approaches, procedures, method and standards, whereas project implementation concerns with the actual installation process as well as the quality of the installation.

because we care

Educating our people and our local area. Here at The Ford Engineering Group, we take pride in our corporate responsibility, and we regularly partake in activities which we feel will benefit Tyneside.
